Pushdown Rising Star Ad

The Pushdown is a large pushdown teaser running the full width of the page with expandability.

A unit that displays a teaser image/swf initially. This type of expansion affects the entire unit. A teaser image/swf is shown until the user hovers over/clicks on the expansion button.

The full unit then slides vertically or horizontally into view from the edge of the teaser, in the direction the arrow in the expansion button was pointing toward.
