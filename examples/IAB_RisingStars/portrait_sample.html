Portrait Rising Star Ad

The Portrait is a side displaying advertisement with individual roll out overlays.

A large app will expand horizontally while maintaining the height of its initial state. The height of the asset (in this example a video) will vary depending on whether a title for the app is set and the number of assets the app contains.

Clicking on "App" areas in the ad cause them to expand out into the content to display videos or enhanced functionality.
